# Research Library

## Purpose

The Research Library serves as the scholarly foundation of the Educator Intelligence Platform.

This repository is not intended to function as a traditional bibliography. Instead, it is a living collection of research, annotations, literature reviews, and design insights that inform the philosophy, architecture, and implementation of the platform.

Research should not simply be collected—it should be interpreted.

Each document focuses on a major discipline that contributes to the project. Individual papers should be summarized, connected to other work, and evaluated for their implications on educational design.

- Feedback and Assessment
- Knowledge Management
- Organizational Behavior
- Artificial Intelligence in Education
- Music Education
- Human–Computer Interaction
