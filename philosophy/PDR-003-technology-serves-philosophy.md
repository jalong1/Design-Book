PDR-003

Technology Serves Philosophy

Status: Accepted

Decision

Educational philosophy shall guide technological implementation.

Technology shall never define the philosophical direction of the project.

Rationale

Technological systems inevitably change.

Educational philosophy endures.

The value of the Educator Intelligence Platform should remain intact even if every implementation technology is replaced.

Hermes is one implementation.

The philosophy transcends Hermes.

Consequences

* Chapters remain understandable without implementation details.
* Architectural decisions emerge from educational principles.
* Technologies may evolve without altering the project’s philosophical foundation.
