PDR-001

The Humanity Test

Status: Accepted

Decision

Every significant philosophical, architectural, and implementation decision shall be evaluated according to a single guiding question:

Does this ultimately help educators remain more present for the people they teach?

Rationale

Technology inevitably shapes the allocation of educators’ attention.

The purpose of the Educator Intelligence Platform is not technological sophistication, automation, or productivity alone.

Its purpose is to strengthen human relationships by reducing unnecessary cognitive and administrative burden.

Every design decision should therefore be evaluated according to whether it ultimately returns an educator’s attention toward students, scholarship, mentorship, creative activity, and educational community.

Consequences

* Human relationships remain the project’s highest priority.
* Efficiency is valuable only when it creates greater pedagogical presence.
* Features that increase complexity without increasing human presence should be reconsidered.
* Philosophy precedes implementation.
