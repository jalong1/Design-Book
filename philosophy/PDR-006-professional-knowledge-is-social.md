PDR-006

Professional Knowledge is Social

Status: Accepted

Decision

The project recognizes professional knowledge as simultaneously individual, communal, and historical.

Rationale

No educator develops alone.

Professional judgment emerges through relationships with students, mentors, colleagues, scholars, institutions, and the broader educational community.

Educational infrastructure should strengthen these relationships rather than isolate educators from them.

Consequences

* Community becomes a core design principle.
* Educational systems should facilitate collaboration and shared learning.
* Individual reflection remains connected to collective educational practice.
* The platform supports educators; educators support one another.
