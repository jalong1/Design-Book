PDR-004

Professional Judgment as the Central Asset

Status: Accepted

Decision

Professional judgment is recognized as one of an educator’s greatest professional assets.

Rationale

Teaching requires continual professional judgment developed through learning, reflection, experience, and adaptation.

Documents, lesson plans, and assessments are valuable.

Their educational significance emerges from the professional judgment that created them.

Supporting educators therefore requires supporting the development and stewardship of professional judgment.

Consequences

* Professional judgment becomes a recurring concept throughout the project.
* Educational systems should preserve context in addition to artifacts.
* Reflection becomes a first-class design concern.
