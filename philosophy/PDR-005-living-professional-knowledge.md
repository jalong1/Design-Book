PDR-005

Living Professional Knowledge

Status: Accepted

Decision

Professional knowledge shall be treated as a living and evolving body of work.

Rationale

Professional knowledge accumulates through continual practice.

It should remain connected across semesters, institutions, scholarly activity, and professional responsibilities.

Knowledge erosion occurs when context becomes separated from experience.

Educational infrastructure should reduce this erosion while supporting continual growth.

Consequences

* Systems should prioritize continuity.
* Context should be preserved alongside artifacts.
* Reflection should connect past work with future practice.
* Every semester should strengthen the next.
